Why Choose Villa Tullia

Nestled in a tranquil corner of Padenghe sul Garda, Villa Tullia offers a perfect blend of modern comfort and lakeside charm. With sweeping views of Lake Garda, a private garden, and elegant interiors, it's your idyllic home away from home. The villa sleeps up to 8 guests across four bedrooms and includes all the amenities to make your stay effortless and memorable.

Breathtaking Lake View

From sunrise to sunset, the villa offers an uninterrupted panorama of the lake, where colors change with the hours of the day. Enjoy your morning coffee as mist rises off the water, or sip a glass of local wine while the sun sets behind the hills. It’s the kind of view you never get tired of.

Cozy Interiors

Step inside and feel instantly at home. Villa Tullia’s interiors combine warmth and elegance: soft lighting, comfortable furniture, and thoughtful details designed for relaxation. Whether you’re gathered with family for dinner or curled up with a book by the fireplace, every corner invites you to slow down.

BBQ & Outdoor Dining

Gather your loved ones and fire up the grill. The villa’s outdoor BBQ area is perfect for long summer evenings filled with laughter, delicious food, and the scent of herbs from the garden. Dining al fresco beneath the stars is an experience you’ll treasure long after your stay.

Private Garden

Escape to your own green sanctuary. The villa’s private garden is both peaceful and inviting — a place where you can sunbathe, read under a shady tree, or simply breathe in the fresh air. It’s the perfect balance of privacy and beauty, designed to make your holiday truly restful.
